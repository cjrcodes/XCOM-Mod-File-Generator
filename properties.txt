XComGame file properties:

    - Default body part template instance:
            [(TemplateName) X2BodyPartTemplate]
            DisplayName="(TemplateName)"

    - Custom body part template instance:
            [(Insert) X2BodyPartTemplate]
            DisplayName=""

Number of templates generated:
    - # of enabled PartTypes * # of enabled ArmorTemplates * # of enabled genders
    - 10 * 16 * 2 = 320 maximum
    - Head = 1 minimum (No genders, 1 gender), 2 maximum (Both genders)
    - Voice = 1 minimum (No genders, 1 gender), 2 maximum (Both genders)
    - Head Props = 1 minimum (No genders, 1 gender), 2 maximum (Both genders)
    - Body/Body Extras = 1 minimum (No genders, 1 gender), 320 maximum (both genders selected, all body/body extra parts selected, all armor templates selected)
